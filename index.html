About Me

I was born in Italy. A small town outside of Verona. This began my love for coffee! I currently reside in Nashville, TN. With the booming growth of the city, we now have a plethora of coffee shops. Though I am not a coffee shop blogger, my goal is to try to visit each local coffee shop. I'm not quite sure if I have a favorite so far because each shop offers it's own "specialties". I do enjoy taking pictures of each crafted creation made by baristas!
